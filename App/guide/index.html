Pink means that the position is right and the letter is in the right family. However, the letter played is not the exact letter used in the word because it has a different ending vowel (e.g. ኢ instead of እ).

Gray means neither the letter nor any letter in its family are part of the word.

Blue means that the position is wrong but the letter is in the right family. Like pink, blue tells you that the letter played is not an exact match but belongs to the same letter family (in this case, ዮ is played instead of የ).

The game ends when all the letters are correct. You do not need to use all the clues provided in previous guesses in your next guess. In fact, sometimes it can be helpful to ignore some of the clues to try and get more useful clues using other words.

A new ቃልነት will be released every day at midnight East African Time (EAT/UTC+3).
